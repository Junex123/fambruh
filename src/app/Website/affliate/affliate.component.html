Affiliate Program - FAQs

What is the Sizzix Affiliate Program?

The Sizzix Affiliate Program provides you with commission for sales generated from your website. We provide you with custom links and banners that you can post. When a visitor clicks to sizzix.com and makes a purchase, we track it and give you a commission on the sale. It’s a great way to earn extra money for your website. To learn more about Affiliate Marketing, visit General Affiliate Marketing for Beginners Overview.

Is my website eligible for the Affiliate Program?

Websites that are relevant to our product offering and do not contain unlawful or indecent content are eligible. We reserve the right to decline membership or revoke a membership at any time at our sole discretion.

Can I become an affiliate if I live outside of the United States?

Yes. We accept affiliates from anywhere in the world, as long as ShareASale supports your country of residence. Note: sizzix.com only ships to the United States.

How do I sign up?

The Sizzix Affiliate Program is administered through ShareASale, our third-party affiliate network. To join, you must first become a member of the ShareASale Affiliate Network. You can join both our program and ShareASale network in one step here. There is no cost to join or fees associated with the Sizzix Affiliate Program. Quick Tip: Use an email address that matches the website in your profile (i.e. marywebsite.com and www.website.com) to process your application quicker.

Once your application has been received, you will receive a confirmation email via ShareASale. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder. Once approved, you will receive a welcome email from Sizzix.

How do I get links or banners to Sizzix?

After you apply and are accepted to our Affiliate Program, visit the Sizzix ShareASale Affiliate Page. There, you can browse through the library of text and banner links. Navigate to “Get Links” and click "Get a link/banner." Look for Sizzix, then click "Get Link", which is located below our company name. When you add a text link, banner or product to your website, it will be tagged with your unique affiliate ID. There is neither a minimum nor any limit to how many links or banners you should place on your website. We have found that posting a creative banner along with a text link improves click-through and conversion rates.

What is the cookie duration?

Sizzix cookies last for 60 days. This mean that you get credit for any referred customers who return to the site within 60 days, even if they do so directly.

What is the average order size?

The average order size at sizzix.com is $89.

Can I use graphics and images from Sizzix?

The entire Sizzix website and its content are protected by copyright. You may use images and content from our websites in the promotion of our products on your own website; however, all images must be used in the sale of the product through the affiliate program and attributed to us. If you have a special circumstance that requires the use of any graphics, product images, or content in a manner that is not consistent with the above, you must contact us for written prior approval to use.

When and how do I receive payment?

ShareASale will pay your monthly commission by either check or direct deposit around the end of the following month. In order to receive payment, your account needs to have a balance of $50 of more before the end of the month. If that $50 minimum is not reached, the balance will continue to roll forward into the next month.

What sales and revenue reports does ShareASale provide?

Each transaction is logged on ShareASale servers and available for review in detail. ShareASale will provide real-time tracking of your click, sales, and commissions. Please do not contact Sizzix for this information as we do not have access to your account.
